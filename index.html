About

Studio Hobi is a design studio based in the Catskills, working with clients in New York City and beyond. Founded by Nick Simmons and Mara Lookabaugh, the studio specializes in custom interiors and one-of-a-kind furniture — spaces and objects designed to feel considered, quiet, and built to last.
